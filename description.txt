CUBESAT
 1. Power-Management Sub-System:
      This sub-system is designed to manage power related activities in the cubesat, basically it monitors and 
      regulates battery usage by other sub-systems.

 2. Input-Management Sub-System
      This sub-system is designed to manage sensor related activities in the cubesat, basically it monitors all 
      sensors(when to activate a sensor and when to put it to sleep)

 3. Output-Management Sub-System
      This sub-system is designed to manage communication and storage related activities is the cubesat, basically
      it manages SD card writes and data transmission.
